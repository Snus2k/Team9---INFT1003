CONTROLS / HOW TO PLAY

  • Keyboard is required

  • Acquire as many points as possible within the time limit

  • Gain points by hitting the corresponding keys on the keyboard that the virus inhabit

  • The mission is over when time runs out




There are four different types of virus with their own effects on hit:

+ EARN EXTRA POINTS

+ EARN EXTRA TIME

- LOSE POINTS

Toggle RISK mode ON or OFF:

When ON, virus appear faster and earned/lost points are doubled






Good luck on your mission.
